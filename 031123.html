This is my first note on my website. I have realized that I rant for a long time all the time about people owning things themselves and how we should all take more creative control of things in our lives. I will no longer be a hypocrite and i will actually do things with this more often. I was up in Maine the last few days staying on the coast with my girlfriend Lola. We drove back this morning and stopped at what my friends and I have begun to refer to as a "dumb store". A dumb store is any store that you walk into and you imediately feel that an $18 pair of blue squiggly candles would not be out of place. They are owned and operated almost in entirely by milenials who when they were 23 had "I mustache you a question" tattoos on their fingers. I do love these stores. I got a butter holder and a new wine opener. We walked around doing nothing, went to the same restaurant twice since nothing was open, and watched Silver Linings Playbook. It was an incredible weekend.
